## Why Buck?

Buck provides deterministic builds, fine-grained dependency tracking, and significant performance improvements over traditional Xcode builds. This sample project shows you how to structure your iOS app to take full advantage of Buck's capabilities while maintaining compatibility with Xcode workflows.
